# FIRST safety materials
FIRST has a couple public safety materials created in partnership with U.L. All official Safety materials can be found on the [Safety page](https://www.firstinspires.org/resources/library/safety) on FIRSTs website.


## Official Safety Manual

FIRST publishes a new Safety Manual every year. The manual typically does not change that much year to year but you should always look at the new one to keep up to date with any new safety protocols. 
The manual contains various topics but all of them are in the context of FIRST in the shop/lab and at competition. It also has some resources such as a Safety Checklist, <mark>Incident Report Sheets</mark>, and more.

## Safety Learning Portal
The FIRST Safety Learning Portal is a site with 7 different courses about different safety topics. It is a great resource for learning about the topics. The courses are the following:
- Fire Extinguisher Safety Awareness
- Hand and Power Tool Safety Awareness
- Hazard Communication - Safety Data Sheets (US)
- Hearing Conservation Awareness
- Lockout/Tagout/Tryout (LOTOTO) Awareness
- Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) Awareness
- Recognizing Electrical Hazards

These courses are aimed more about general safety rather than in a context specific to FIRST.

## Competition Injures
When an injury occurs at competition you are supposed to fill out an incident report sheet for FIRST. This is so FIRST can track how people get injured at competitions and try and fix them for the future.

## Youth Protection Program (YPP)
If there is a YPP volition or concern FIRST has a different form that you can fill out. This allows you to tell FIRST what is going on and what kind of assistance you would like.
