# Competition Safety

Competition is an extreemly fun expreience but also comes with many things to keep in mind. 

## Pits

The pits are where robots are when not on the field and where all the tools are that teams use at competition. It can be a very chatic place expessally when something when not planned. There are many rules in the pits (many of which are the same as machine shops). Some rules are constant for example no matter what is happening, safety glasses are required at **all times** no matter what is going on. The full set of rules can be found in section 7.3 of the offical FIRST Safety Manual

### Pit Admin

Pit Admin is the source of just about any bit of information at competition. If you have questions about anything, they will know the answer, or they will get you to the person that will know the right answer. There are some things that you are supposed to go to them for. For example if there is a battery spill you should go to Pit Admin so they can contact event managment incase the venue has something special that needs to be done.
