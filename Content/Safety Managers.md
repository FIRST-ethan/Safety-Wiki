# Safety Managers

Safety Managers are volunteers at competitions that are in charge of safety. Almost anything to do with safety at competition you can go to them to talk to, ask questions, etc. The only thing that you should not go to them for is injuries. Go directly to the onsite <mark>Public Safety/EMS</mark> as they have all the proper training, knowledge and equipment needed to help. 

Some of the responsibilities of Safety Managers are:
- Running the daily [Safety Captain](Safety%20Captains.md) meeting
- Awarding the <mark>Safety All Star award</mark>
- Making sure everyone entering the pits have safety glasses/goggles
- Working with Safety Captains to make sure everyone is being safe
