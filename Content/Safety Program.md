# Safety Program

A Safety program is a collection of trainings, documentation, etc that an organization (such as a team) has that has to do with Safety.

## Why Having a Safety Program is Importent

Having a Safety Program is important to create to decrease the incident rate on your team. It can also make members of your team fell more comfortable working with power tools and machines, even with how dangerous they can be.

## How to Create a Safety Program

Safety Programs come in all shapes and sizes, and every program starts small. It is **100% OK** if your program only has a few items included as you can always build on top of what you have.

### Training

Trainings are ways to teach others how to handle different incidents. That may include CPR and/or First Aid training, training on how to fill out an incident report, and more. These are important so many people know how to respond and not only the Safety Captains and Mentors.

Trainings come in many forms, they can me an interactive slide show, hands on, a paper worksheet, etc. There are many that already exist that you can use such as the [FIRST Safety Learning Portal](Content/FIRST%20materials.md#Safety%20Learning%20Portal).

### Documentation

Documentation is a huge category of safety materials. This can include Material Safety Data Sheet (MSDS), Safety manuals, Incident report sheets and more.

#### Material Safety Data Sheet (MSDS)

MSDS is documentation from manufactures that contain information about a product. They include many things such as name, composition, how to handle fires, and more. There is more info about them in the course about them on the FIRST Safety Learning Portal.

#### Safety manuals

Many teams have custom Safety Manuals that include items that are specific to their team such as specific circumstances in their own machine shop/lab or specific procedures required by their school

FIRST also as their official one that can be found on their [Safety page](https://www.firstinspires.org/resources/library/safety)

#### Incident Report Sheets

Incident Report Sheets (Report Sheets) is a document to keep track of incidents that have happened. They are useful as they can track how incidents occur that we humans may not notice without them. Armed with that knolage you can then modify procedures to prevent future incidents.
