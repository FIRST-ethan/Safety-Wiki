# Safety Captains

(The) Safety Captain(s) are student(s) on each team that help the [Safety Managers](https://github.com/POBots-353/Safety-Wiki/blob/main/Content/Safety%20Managers.md) by making sure their team is being safe. This includes making sure people are wearing proper [Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)](https://github.com/POBots-353/Safety-Wiki/blob/main/Content/Personal%20Protective%20Equitment%20(PPE).md), and other safety things that Safety Managers pass along at the Daily Meetings.

Safety Captains should be a pioneer of safety in the shop/lab and at competition (not just only in the pit). They should be on the look out for any unsafe behavior, hazards, etc and correct the hazard and teach the involved parties. They should also develop more safety related stuff such as <mark>Safety Programs</mark>, Materials, Precations, etc.

Teams should have at least 1 safety captain but more than one is better. A single person can only oversee so much of your team's shop/lab at once or be there (or the pit at completion) at once as breaks are important.
